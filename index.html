OUR STORY

A few years back, God gave me a project to work on. Although I knew I was meant to undertake it, I was scared to start and doubted my capacity to handle such a significant task, even though I had already navigated similar experiences. Fear held me back, making me question whether I could truly succeed. Over time, I realized that God would never assign me a huge project without equipping me with the necessary abilities. Last year, in 2023, I met Mr. @tunde_rhema, the CEO of @tr_leadershiphub. He gave me a scholarship for one of their programs. Despite having other business ventures that needed clarification, this project was the only one that felt right to focus on. Together, we developed a blueprint outlining how the project could work and identified the key elements to include. Even after everything was prepared, I found many reasons not to start because fear crept in again. I began to ask myself if I was truly ready, so I left the project. About two months ago, the Holy Spirit started reminding me of the project I had neglected, and it became a burden on my heart. Just three weeks ago, during a service at Menorah, while our pastor was ministering, I heard the Holy Spirit speak to me in a clear voice, as if He were sitting right next to me. He said, "I took you through that process to help others." My heart melted, and I was broken. At that moment, I knew I couldn't run anymore. Right then and there, I began to ask for strength because I knew I couldn't do it all by myself. We are a community whose foundation is built on God's instructions to help people through the pain and process that he took us through.
